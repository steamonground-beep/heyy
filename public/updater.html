How Auto Updater Works

1

You Publish an Update

Enter the new version number and a direct download link to a .zip or .exe file. Click Publish Update.

↓
2

App Checks on Startup

When UABEANext launches, it contacts heyy-rose.vercel.app/api/update and compares the published version with its current version.

↓
3

Update Dialog Shown

If a newer version is found, the app shows a dialog with your release notes and a Download & Install button.

↓
4

Install & Restart

The app downloads the file, runs it (or extracts the zip into its folder), deletes the old files, then relaunches automatically.
